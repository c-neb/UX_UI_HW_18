User Research

I am open to whatever approach yields the best results. Utilizing surveys, user personas, usability testing, user interviews, and card sorting are examples of methods I use.

User Empathy

Without empathy, there is no point in design. One must ask questions, be an active listener, and be open minded to the responses to receive valuable data.

Qualitative Interviews

To conduct a good qualitative interview, questions should be open-ended, responses should be neutral. And should start with simple questions that lead into more complex questions.
